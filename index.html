Best Christian Movies of 2022

Positive entertainment that changes lives, inspires hearts, and lifts the spirit.

Tyson's Run

A 15-year-old autistic boy, in public school for the first time, tries to win his father's approval by running his first marathon. To achieve his goal, he'll be trained by a champion marathon runner

This a wonderful story about persistence and that whatever you dream comes true if you try hard.

The Unbreakable Boy

An autistic boy with a rare bone disease inspires everyone around him with his unshakable faith and remarkable outlook on life.

An ispirational movie of all the times. Faith in what you don't see, but it can be reachable if you believe on it.

Run the Race

Two desperate brothers sacrifice today for a better tomorrow. Reeling from his mother's death and his father's abandonment, Zach, an All-State athlete, finds glory on the football field, working to earn a college scholarship and the brothers' ticket out of town..

Nothing can stop you, if you persist and believe. As a believer in Jesus Christ, you run a race. It, too, is the race of a lifetime. It, too, dominates your mind. It, too, occupies your waking moments. It, too, becomes the central focus of your existence.

Run the Race

Unlike many faith-based films that have come before it, Run the Race is unabashedly unafraid to deliver a message of trusting God in the midst of all circumstances, not just some.
